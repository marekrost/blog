Hello and welcome to my blog. I am a technology enthusiast based in Prague, driven by a lifelong passion for understanding how things work and if they could work better: a microcontroller, a complex marketing algorithm or a generalized process.

By day, I lead development to build robust digital infrastructures. By night (and on weekends), I am a tinkerer and a critic of the "move fast and break things" culture when it comes to privacy and software stability.

I am co-founder of companies Triton IT, WebMedea services and BGU.
